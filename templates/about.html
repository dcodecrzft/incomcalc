About This Tool

What is this tool?

This app is tool which you can use to predict your income and poverty status based on 1: the length of your education in years and 2: How many hours you work per week.
This app uses a Machine Learning model to predict the result. (Specifically, a linear regression model based on scikit-learn)

How do I use this app?

After heading to Home > Calculate, you'll find the interface in which you can input your details. You need to input the duration of your education in years
and how many hours you work per week. Note that they have to be whole numbers. Once you click submit, you'll be taken to a page where you can see the result. If the result is equal to 0,
then your income is likely to be >$50K and there is low liklihood of you being in poverty. Whereas if the result is equal to 1, then your income is likely to be <=$50K and there is
higher liklihood of you being in poverty.

How does it work?

Basically how this works is that the AI uses a linear regression model to predict the your income based on based you level of education and hours per week.
The model trains itself off of a small dataset and based on the patterns it finds, predicts how much income you may have.
